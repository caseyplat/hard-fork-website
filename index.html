Meet Your Hosts

Two journalists making sense of tech, one podcast at a time

CN

Casey Newton

Founder, Platformer

The handsome one. Casey is the founder and editor of Platformer, a publication about the intersection of tech and democracy trusted by over 200,000 weekly readers. He spent a decade covering Silicon Valley for The Verge, CNET, and the San Francisco Chronicle. His reporting was a finalist for the National Magazine Award. A proud Northwestern journalism grad, Casey brings sharp analysis and occasional chaos to every episode.

Platformer Twitter
KR

Kevin Roose

Tech Columnist, The New York Times

The one who talks to chatbots. Kevin is a technology columnist at The New York Times, where he writes "The Shift" examining tech, business, and culture. He previously created Rabbit Hole, a Peabody Award finalist about internet radicalization. He's the author of three books including Futureproof, and his conversation with Bing's "Sydney" made headlines worldwide. His fourth book on A.G.I. drops in 2026. Brown grad. Robot vacuum enthusiast.
